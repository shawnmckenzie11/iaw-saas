# IAW Courier: Driver Delivery App Instructions

This guide explains how to use the IAW Courier proof-of-delivery (POD) capture application on your smartphone.

---

## 📱 1. Setup & Profile Selection

1. **Open the App**: Launch the app on your mobile browser or home screen.
2. **Select Your Name**: Look at the top-right header of the Dashboard. Tap your initial/profile button to select yourself as the active driver.
   * *Why this matters*: Every pickup waybill you log will be marked with your driver ID for billing and dispatch auditing.

---

## 📥 2. Logging a New Cargo Pickup

Run these steps as soon as you load the cargo into your vehicle:

1. Tap the large blue **➕ NEW PICKUP (WAYBILL)** button on the Dashboard.
2. Fill out the pickup details:
   * **Location/Business Name** (e.g., Wajax, Redpath)
   * **Pickup Address**
   * **Contact Name & Phone** (if available)
3. Enter the Dropoff destination details.
4. Enter the **Parcel Details**:
   * **Description**: What are you carrying? (e.g., "Pipes", "Drill bits")
   * **Quantity**: How many packages?
   * **Weight Category**: Select the matching range (e.g., *Less than 10 lbs*, *11-50 lbs*, etc.).
5. Select the **Vehicle Type** you are driving and the delivery **Priority** (Regular or Rush).
6. Tap **💾 COMPLETE PICKUP & LOG WAYBILL**.
   * *Success*: A unique waybill (e.g., `IAW-20260701-ABCD`) will be generated. The delivery is now logged as **PICKED_UP** and will appear in your active list.

---

## 📤 3. Dropoff & Signature Capture

Run these steps when you arrive at the delivery destination:

1. Locate the waybill under **Active Pickup Logs** on your Dashboard. Tap **SIGN OFF ➡️**.
2. Verify the parcel contents with the recipient.
3. Enter the recipient's **Printed Name** (First and Last name).
4. Tap **📷 CAPTURE PROOF OF DELIVERY PHOTO** to take a photo of the dropped cargo (if required by dispatcher).
5. Hand your phone to the customer. Ask them to:
   * Read the legal receipt statement on screen.
   * Draw their signature clearly in the grey box using their finger.
   * *If they make a mistake*: Tap **❌ Clear Canvas** to redraw.
6. Tap **✔ COMPLETE DELIVERY & SIGN OFF**.
   * *Success*: The delivery is marked **DELIVERED** and moved to your completed history.

---

## 📡 4. Working Offline (No Service / dead zones)

The application is **offline-first**. It is designed to work even if you are out of cell range (e.g., remote mine sites or out-of-town roads).

* **Everything is saved**: You can log pickups and complete signature dropoffs without an internet connection. The app stores all text, coordinates, and signatures safely on your device.
* **Sync indicators on Dashboard**:
  * **Pending Sync**: Shows deliveries completed while offline that are waiting to upload.
  * **Synced**: Deliveries that have safely reached the central office.
* **Automatic Uploads**: Once your phone reconnects to cellular data or Wi-Fi, the app will upload all pending waybills automatically.
* **⚠️ WARNING**: Do **NOT** log out, close the browser private tab, or clear your browser cache/storage until your sync queue shows **0 Pending Sync** and everything is marked **✓ SYNCED**. Doing so will delete offline files that have not yet uploaded.
